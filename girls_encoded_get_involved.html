Students

Are you a high school, college, or graduate student interested in learning more about computer science or meeting other women in technology? We would love to connect with you! Check out some of the events here on our website or send us an e-mail at email@umich.edu to find out more ways to get involved.

Companies

Are you a company interested in supporting women in computer science? We would love for you to partner with us and help us to inspire and encourage more women in technology. There are many ways that you can be involved, from sponsoring a specific event to sending students to conferences. Here are some potential ideas for sponsorship:

  • Sponsor a "Women in Computing" lecture and lunch

  • Send computer science students to the Grace Hopper Conference, the largest gathering of women technologists

  • Host an on-campus event or activity with the Girls Encoded community

  • Sponsor a hackathon

  • Support a computer science class or event at a local Michigan high school

We are interested in working with you to find an exciting opportunity for you to be involved in the computer science community here at the University of Michigan. Please get in touch with us at email@umich.edu to discuss sponsorship or to ask any questions that you might have.
